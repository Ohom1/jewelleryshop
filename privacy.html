Privacy Policy

We are committed to safeguarding your personal information. Learn how we manage your data.

Introduction

At Healet, protecting your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to protect it. By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described herein.

Types of Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Payment and Transaction Details: Information required for processing purchases, such as billing addresses and payment methods.
  • Usage Data: Data about your interactions with our platform, including browsing behavior and preferences.
  • Device Information: Details about the device and browser you use to access our services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To deliver and improve our services and content.
  • To process transactions and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your experience by recommending relevant content.
  • To ensure compliance with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activity.

Security of Your Information

We employ advanced security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Regular audits and updates are conducted to enhance data security.

Sharing Your Information

We only share your data under the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist in our operations (e.g., payment processors, cloud storage).
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our platform, users, or others.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access your data and request copies of the information we hold.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
  • Withdraw consent for certain data processing activities.

Cookies and Tracking

Our platform uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies help us understand your preferences and improve functionality. You can manage cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant updates will be communicated via email or platform notifications.
